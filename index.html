The 12 Steps of FYA

Step 1

We admitted we were powerless over frozen yogurtâ€”that our lives had become a swirly mess.

Step 2

Came to believe that a Power greater than ourselves could restore our brain freeze to sanity.

Step 3

Made a decision to turn our toppings and our lives over to the care of a higher power, as we understood them (not the sprinkle shaker).

Step 4

Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of our froyo receipts.

Step 5

Admitted to the universe, to ourselves, and to another human being the exact nature of our toppings addiction.

Step 6

Were entirely ready to have our Higher Power remove all these defective self-serve techniques.

Step 7

Humbly asked the universe to remove our shortcomings (and our punch card collections).

Step 8

Made a list of all persons we had enabled with "just one more topping," and became willing to make amends to them all.

Step 9

Made direct amends to such people wherever possible, except when to do so would cause them to relapse at the toppings bar.

Step 10

Continued to take personal inventory and when we were weighing our cups wrong, promptly admitted it.

Step 11

Sought through meditation and prayer to improve our conscious contact with our Higher Power, praying only for knowledge of their will for us and the power to carry that out (without stopping at FroYo shops).

Step 12

Having had a spiritual awakening as the result of these steps, we tried to carry this message to frozen yogurt addicts and to practice these principles in all our dessert affairs.
